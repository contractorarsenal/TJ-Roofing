Common Questions

Got questions?

How much does a new roof cost in Olympia, WA? +
Pricing depends on roof size, slope, material, and condition of the existing structure. TJ Roofing provides free estimates so you know exactly what you're looking at before any work begins. Contact us to schedule your visit.
Are you licensed and insured in Washington State? +
Yes. TJ Roofing LLC is fully licensed and insured in Washington State. We carry the coverage required to protect your property and our team on every job.
Do you do reroofing or only new construction? +
We handle both. Whether you need a roof for a new build, a complete reroof on an existing home, or repairs and gutter work — our team is experienced across all of it.
What types of roofing materials do you install? +
We work with a variety of roofing systems and materials to match your home's needs and your budget. We'll help you select the right option during your free estimate visit.
How long does a reroof take? +
Most residential reroofs are completed in 1–3 days depending on the size and complexity of your roof. We give you a clear timeline upfront so there are no surprises.
